MY FAVOURITE MEAL: BEANS PORRIDGE

About

Beans were an important source of protein and they are gotten from the FABACEAE family known as legume plant family.The incredible diversity of beans encompasses many unique varieties. Beans porridge is a popular Nigeria local dish that is made from beans and is suitable to eat with cassava flakes, bread or local fermented corn starch porridge(ogi).

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Honey beans/Nigeria brown beans
  • 1/4 cup of palm oil
  • 4 Roma tomatoes
  • 2 big bulb of onions
  • 1 large ball pepper
  • 5 tbsp Ground dried shrimp(optional)
  • 3 tbsp Shrimp bullion
  • Salt to taste

Steps/Procedures

  1. Rinse the beans and place in a deep pot with 7 cups of water
  2. Boil the beans on medium heat until it becomes soft, it could take about 40 minutes.
  3. While the beans are boiling, blend the tomatoes, peppers and onions and set aside
  4. Once the beans are softened from boiling, pour the blended tomatoes into the pot
  5. Allow the beans to cook for an additional 10mins with the tomatoes, then add in the crayfish bullion and palm oil.Stir,and cook for another 10mins
  6. Season the beans with salt to your tast.Turn off the heat, then allow it to cool before serving
